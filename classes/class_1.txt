Class: Hamburger

Attributes:
* pattyCount (integer)
* cheese (boolean)
* internalTemp (string)
* bun (string)


addPatties (modifies the number of patties on the hamburger. This changes the pattyCount attribute)
addCheese (boolean that adds cheese to the hamburger if true. This modifies the cheese attribute)
changeCookTemp (modifies the cook temperature of the hamburger. Such as Rare, Medium-Rare, Medium, etc. This modifies the internalTemp attribute)
changeBun (changes the type of bun used on the hamburger. Such as plain or sesame bun. This modifies the bun attribute)
